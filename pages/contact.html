The Silk Mark is a quality label issued by the Central Silk Board, Government of India. It certifies that the product contains 100% natural silk — no artificial or synthetic fibers. Every ANR silk garment carries this certification.

Absolutely. We accept custom weaving orders for both personal and wholesale customers. Custom orders typically require 10–21 days depending on complexity and quantity. Contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a timeline and quote.

Minimum order quantities vary by product: Silk Shirts start at 10 pieces, Dhotis/Vesti at 6 pieces, and Bridal Sets at 5 sets. For mixed orders, we're flexible — just reach out and we'll work something out.

Always dry clean your silk. If hand washing is necessary, use cold water and mild soap gently — never wring. Dry in shade only. Store folded in muslin or cotton cloth away from moisture and perfume. Steam iron on low heat from the inside.
